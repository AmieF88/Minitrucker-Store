ABOUT US

Kirk & Amie


Just two fun people who fell in love with the custom vehicle scene… and then with each other! Kirk, a full-time teacher, was born and raised in south Louisiana, while Amie, a student, was all the way across the country in California. They first met by trading vehicles through Facebook Marketplace — what a small world! They later reconnected and jumped into the car show scene, full throttle. In 2023, they launched their new business and have been rolling ever since!

What we do


We are first and foremost a brand built for people who love custom rides, bold designs, and standing out from the crowd. Our shop offers a wide variety of unique artwork and products made to celebrate all things vehicle-related — from lifted trucks to sleek street builds and everything in between.
You can find us traveling across the map to car shows, meets, and special events, connecting with the community and showcasing our latest designs. We also offer most of our products online, making it easy for anyone to support the brand from anywhere.
Customization is at the heart of what we do. Whether you’re looking for engraved cups, custom hoodies, personalized merch for your crew, or branded gear for your company, we’ve got you covered. If you dream it, we can create it. Driven by passion and fueled by creativity — we’re here to bring your ideas to life and help you represent your build, your club, and your style with pride.
